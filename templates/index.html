Instructions

  • Pregnancies: Enter the number of times you have been pregnant (0 if male).
  • Glucose Level: Enter fasting glucose level (mg/dL). Normal: 70-99 mg/dL, Prediabetes: 100-125 mg/dL, Diabetes: 126+ mg/dL.
  • Blood Pressure: Enter your diastolic blood pressure (mm Hg).
  • Skin Thickness: Enter skin fold thickness (mm).
  • Insulin Level: Enter insulin concentration (mu U/ml).
    Healthy: 2-20 mu U/ml, Prediabetes: 21-35 mu U/ml, Diabetes: 36+ mu U/ml.
  • BMI: Enter your Body Mass Index.
  • Diabetes Pedigree Function: A value indicating genetic influence.
  • Age: Enter your age in years.
  • Exercise Level: Enter 1 if you exercise regularly, otherwise 0.
  • Family History: Enter 1 if there is a family history of diabetes, otherwise 0.
  • Diet Type: Enter 1 for a balanced diet, otherwise 0.
  • UL Code: A unique identifier for tracking patient history in the system.
